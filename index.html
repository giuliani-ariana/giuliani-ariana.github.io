About

As a graphic designer with over 10 years of experience in web and interface design, I focus on creating work that is both visually compelling and easy to use. I have developed custom themes and skins for applications like Obsidian, VS Code, and Spotify, which I share for free through GitHub so others can use and build on them.

I am currently studying an Arts & Science university program with a focus on graphic design courses. My work always prioritizes clarity, functionality, and communication while maintaining a strong sense of aesthetics and atmosphere.
