About

I'm a graphic designer with over 10 years of experience in web and interface design, focused on creating work that is both visually compelling and easy to use. I've developed custom themes and skins for applications like Obsidian, Cockatrice, and Spotify, which I share for free through GitHub so others can use and build on them.

Currently, I'm studying an Arts & Science university program with a focus on graphic design courses. My work always prioritizes clarity, functionality, and communication while maintaining a strong sense of aesthetics and atmosphere.
